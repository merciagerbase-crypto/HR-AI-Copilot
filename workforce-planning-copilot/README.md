# Workforce Planning Copilot

An interactive prototype for an HR, Finance and business leadership workforce review.

## Core user problem
Workforce planning often lives in disconnected spreadsheets, discussions start with conflicting numbers and decisions are made without a clear view of the trade-offs between headcount, cost, attrition, vacancy risk and succession coverage.

## Product approach
- Create a common view from approved aggregate workforce data
- Identify material signals and explain why they matter
- Run specific decision scenarios
- Separate observed data, assumptions and recommendations
- Produce prioritized actions with owners and timing
- Keep final budget and people decisions with accountable leaders

## Production safeguards
- Aggregate or de-identified data by default
- Approved source systems and data quality checks
- Role-based access controls
- Model outputs grounded in cited metrics
- Human review before any executive recommendation is distributed
