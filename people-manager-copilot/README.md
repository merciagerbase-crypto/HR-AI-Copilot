# People Manager Copilot

An interactive prototype for managers who need to prepare for performance, promotion, conflict or compensation conversations.

## Core user problem
Managers often arrive at sensitive people conversations without a clear structure, incomplete facts and limited awareness of escalation triggers. HR then receives poorly prepared cases and inconsistent documentation.

## Product approach
- Ask for structured work-related context
- Generate a case summary and missing-information checklist
- Provide a neutral conversation opening and questions
- Suggest a practical follow-up plan
- Flag specialist review for high-risk topics
- Show clear decision boundaries

## Production safeguards
- HR policy retrieval and citations
- Human review on sensitive cases
- Approved data sources only
- Audit log and versioning
- No autonomous promotion, termination, compensation or disciplinary decisions
