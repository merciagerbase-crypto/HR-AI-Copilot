# Success metrics

## People Manager Copilot

- Manager preparation time for sensitive conversations
- Manager confidence before and after using the workflow
- Percentage of cases escalated to the right expert function
- Repeat question reduction for the HR team
- Human-review completion for high-impact workflows
- Safety incidents, complaints and overridden recommendations

## Workforce Planning Copilot

- Time required to create a monthly workforce recommendation
- Percentage of high-priority roles with a documented decision
- Forecast accuracy by role family or business unit
- Vacancy aging in strategic roles
- Attrition in priority populations
- Payroll variance versus plan
- Decision adoption rate and post-decision outcome quality
